What this is

I track room availability on major booking platforms (Marriott, IHG, Hilton, Choice, direct sites) to estimate city-wide occupancy, updated three times daily.

Traditional occupancy data comes from monthly reports 30-60 days after the fact. This system tracks booking velocity in real-time to spot patterns as they develop; useful for catching mid-week event spikes or faster-than-normal compression before availability tightens.

I'm currently validating forecast accuracy and looking for feedback from DMOs and tourism planners.

Built by a former hotel operator who retrained as a software developer and saw a gap worth filling.
